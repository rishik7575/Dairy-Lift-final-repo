The SmartDairy Concept

SmartDairy is a platform that revolutionizes the dairy farming industry by combining traditional dairy production with modern technology, sustainable practices, and innovative investment opportunities.

Our platform allows investors to own dairy cattle remotely, track their production and health in real-time, and receive returns from multiple revenue streams: milk production, biogas generation from waste, and fertilizer production.

Through our integrated waste-to-energy systems, we're closing the loop on dairy farm operations, creating a more sustainable and profitable model for both investors and the environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment starts at $1,000 for fractional cattle ownership. For full cattle ownership, investments typically start at $2,500 depending on breed and age.

How are returns distributed?

Returns are distributed monthly based on milk production, biogas generation, and fertilizer sales. You can choose to reinvest your returns or have them deposited directly to your bank account.

What happens if a cow gets sick or dies?

All cattle in our program are insured against illness and death. In case of such events, you'll be compensated according to the insurance policy, or you can choose to reinvest in another cow without additional costs.

Can I visit the farm and see my cattle?

Yes! We organize quarterly farm visits for investors to meet their cattle and see the operations. Virtual farm tours are also available monthly through our platform.

How liquid is my investment?

You can sell your investment back to SmartDairy or to other investors on our secondary market at any time, subject to a minimum holding period of 6 months. Market conditions may affect the selling price.

How sustainable are your farming practices?

Our farms follow organic and regenerative farming practices. The biogas system reduces methane emissions, and our energy production offsets farm operations. We're certified carbon-neutral through our offsetting program.
